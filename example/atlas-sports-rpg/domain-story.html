Domain

The narrative engine: it turns the simulation’s raw events into a living story of arcs, memory, news, and identity.

OwnsThe whole narrative pipeline — observing state, multi-tier memory, spawning & resolving story arcs, resolving decision moments, generating prose, assembling news, and building retrospectives.
WhyIt makes transient gameplay into a history a player cares about: rivalries, slumps, redemption arcs, franchise reputations that persist across seasons.
RuleIt is a pure observer — it reads sim/world/brain state and writes only narrative artifacts. It never changes the game; effects are queued and applied by their owners.
The load-bearing idea
“A director watching the sim, casting arcs as the season unfolds.”

One orchestrator — SeasonTick — runs every day. It expires tags, flushes the stat observer, rolls vignettes, then ticks the Director, which polls for eligible templates, casts real players into roles, fires beats, and resolves arcs. Memory is written only when a moment passes a salience filter.

The narrative pipeline

event → story

Sim events flow in; the observer emits tags; the Director polls tags to spawn and advance arcs; effects write narrative memory and surface to news and prose.

sim events (read-only)observerstore (tags / memory)director
🎬SeasonTick.dailyTick() is the single entry point, called from simSeason. The Director consumes ArcSeeds queued by sim/brain mutations (a trade queues a beef-former-team seed), casts real players into roles, and dispatches Effects — tag emits, identity contributions, narrative-memory writes.

Data it owns

Narrative artifacts are written onto the world’s entities (Person/Franchise/League) but defined and governed here.

Arc · ArcTemplate · ArcResolutionActive story arcs, their blueprints (roles, beats, resolutions), and the log of how each resolved.
KMEvent · NarrativeMemoryEntryThe cheap event log and the rich, salience-filtered memory entries on each Person/Franchise.
BeefStatePersistent grudges between people (0–100 intensity), e.g. a player traded against their will.
identityTags (14 tags)Per-franchise reputation: spender/frugal, developer, meddler, winning-culture, playoff pedigree…
CachedRetrospectiveVersioned, cache-first career/franchise summaries (eras + defining moments).

Reads (never mutates): the season-event bus & stats from lib/sim; entities from lib/world; archetype & arc-decision logic from lib/brain.
