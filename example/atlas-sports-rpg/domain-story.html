Domain

The narrative engine: it turns the simulation's raw events into a living story of arcs, memory, news, and identity.

OwnsThe whole narrative pipeline — observing state, multi-tier memory, spawning & resolving story arcs, resolving decision moments, generating prose, assembling news, and building retrospectives.
WhyIt makes transient gameplay into a history a player cares about: rivalries, slumps, redemption arcs, franchise reputations that persist across seasons.
RuleIt is a pure observer — it reads sim/world/brain state and writes only narrative artifacts. It never changes the game; effects are queued and applied by their owners.
The load-bearing idea
"A director watching the sim, casting arcs as the season unfolds."

One orchestrator — SeasonTick — runs every day. It expires tags, flushes the stat observer, rolls vignettes, then ticks the Director, which polls for eligible templates, casts real players into roles, fires beats, and resolves arcs. Memory is written only when a moment passes a salience filter.

observer · tags lib/story/observer · tags

↑ back to cards

StatObserver records each game into rolling 5/10/25-game windows and evaluates reactive predicates — slump_candidate, hot_streak, goalie hot/cold — buffering tag emissions. On the day boundary flushTick applies them in canonical order to the TagLayer, a reference-counted store of season- and game-scoped tags, and recomputes prominence for affected people. Tags are the shared currency the Director polls.

memory · persistent lib/story/memory · persistent

↑ back to cards

A two-tier write model. Every narrative moment appends a cheap KMEvent (150 B) to the league's event log; only moments that pass isSalient also write a rich NarrativeMemoryEntry (500–1100 B) onto the relevant Person or Franchise. persistent/ holds longer-lived state — BeefState grudges with intensity that decays daily, pruned on retirement.

director · arcs lib/story/director · arcs

↑ back to cards

The heart of the domain. directorTick runs each day per franchise: it advances active arcs (validate cast → check resolutions → fire beats), then considers new spawns — polling templates by trigger, filtering by cooldowns and the active-arc cap, weighting, and picking one. Casting fills each role from real candidates; if a required role has none, the spawn fails.

An individual arc moves from a mutation-driven ArcSeed through casting, beat-firing (each dispatching Effects), to a resolution logged in arcHistory.

km · decisions lib/story/km · decisions

↑ back to cards

resolveKM is called from the sim loop with a Key-Moment opportunity (clutch shot, fight invitation, goalie pull, …). It looks up the template, resolves the actor's archetype, and picks the archetype-preferred choice — or, in user mode for a user-controlled actor, surfaces a modal. It returns a KMOutcome with the effect for sim to write back.

prose · types-d lib/story/prose · types-d

news lib/story/news

↑ back to cards

assembleFeed gathers candidates (arc beats, resolutions, key moments) and scores each with a multi-factor SalienceBreakdown — surface level, team and subject prominence, freshness, scope, and proximity to the user's team — then ranks them, enforcing per-team and per-template caps. backfillTeamPage tops up a quiet team's feed.

retrospective · identity lib/story/retrospective · identity

↑ back to cards

generateCareerRetrospective is cache-first: it chunks a person's narrative into eras, selects era-balanced defining moments, and (in live mode) prose-renders a lede and legacy. identity/ defines the 14 franchise identity tags (spender, developer, meddler, winning-culture, playoff-pedigree, …) with band boundaries and antagonism pairs; arc/KM effects accumulate per-season contributions that the world's identity tick applies.

Data it owns

Narrative artifacts are written onto the world's entities (Person/Franchise/League) but defined and governed here.

Arc · ArcTemplate · ArcResolutionActive story arcs, their blueprints (roles, beats, resolutions), and the log of how each resolved.
KMEvent · NarrativeMemoryEntryThe cheap event log and the rich, salience-filtered memory entries on each Person/Franchise.
BeefStatePersistent grudges between people (0–100 intensity), e.g. a player traded against their will.
identityTags (14 tags)Per-franchise reputation: spender/frugal, developer, meddler, winning-culture, playoff pedigree…
CachedRetrospectiveVersioned, cache-first career/franchise summaries (eras + defining moments).

Reads (never mutates): the season-event bus & stats from lib/sim; entities from lib/world; archetype & arc-decision logic from lib/brain.
