Tournaments get an admin-set estimated purse that fills in wherever the official ESPN purse is missing

WhatAdd an estimated purse to tournaments, used for live earnings projections and page display until ESPN publishes the real one
WhyESPN often has no purse until tournament week — pages showed nothing and live projected earnings were silently skipped
Risk✓ LOW additive nullable column; official purse always takes precedence, so behavior is unchanged wherever a real purse exists
